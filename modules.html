Modules

Dependencies

  • Flask
  • Flask-SQLAlchemy
  • Flask-WTF
  • WTForms
  • python-dotenv

Key Modules

Flask application and routes (app.py)
Creates and configures the Flask app, initializes extensions, and defines route handlers for home, books, members, and loans, including CRUD and borrow/return actions.
ORM models (models.py)
SQLAlchemy models for Book, Member, and Loan, including relationships between them.
Extensions registry (extensions.py)
Holds shared extension instances (e.g., SQLAlchemy db object) imported by app and models.
Base layout (templates/base.html)
Common HTML shell and navigation; other templates extend this.
Home/index view (templates/index.html)
Landing page with overview and links to books, members, and loans sections.
Books templates (templates/books/*.html)
Implement list, detail, and form screens for book management.
Members templates (templates/members/*.html)
Implement list, detail, and form screens for member management.
Loans templates (templates/loans/*.html)
Implement loan listing and borrowing workflows.
Route and flow tests (tests/test_app.py)
Exercise main endpoints and flows for books, members, and loans.

Data Models

  • Book: Represents library books.
  • Member: Represents patrons.
  • Loan: Ties books and members with loan/return dates and status.
